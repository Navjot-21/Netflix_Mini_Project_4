# 🎬 Netflix Movies & TV Shows Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on the Netflix Movies & TV Shows dataset to uncover meaningful insights about Netflix's content library. The analysis focuses on content distribution, production trends, genres, ratings, movie durations, and geographical distribution using Python and data visualization techniques.

The project demonstrates the complete EDA workflow, including data cleaning, preprocessing, visualization, feature engineering, and business insight generation.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Understand the structure of the Netflix dataset.
- Clean and preprocess the data.
- Analyze Movies and TV Shows distribution.
- Identify top content-producing countries.
- Analyze yearly content release trends.
- Explore maturity ratings and genres.
- Study movie duration patterns.
- Detect outliers using statistical methods.
- Perform feature engineering.
- Generate business insights and recommendations.

## 📈 Key Insights

- Movies account for approximately **69%** of Netflix's content library.
- The **United States** is the largest content-producing country, followed by **India**.
- The majority of Netflix titles were released during the **2010s**.
- **TV-MA** is the most common maturity rating, indicating a strong focus on mature audiences.
- **International Movies**, **Dramas**, and **Comedies** are the most popular genres.
- Most movies have durations between **60 and 120 minutes**.
- The Netflix catalog is primarily composed of recent content released within the last decade.
